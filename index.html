About me

I am currently on a year-long research internship at Brunswick Corporation's I-Jet Lab, my research focuses on visual representation methods for embodied vision specially for 3D understanding of dynamic environments. I completed my master's degree in Computer Science from UIUC, where I helped develop a course track for the data science program on data curation, and worked with Prof. Allen Renear on knowledge represenation techniques in scientifc datasets and semantic reasoning

Broadly, I am interested in computer vision, machine learning, deep learning and knowledge representation techniques.

Before joining the Computer Science Department as a graduate student, I completed my MS in Civil & Environmental Engineering from UIUC. I did my undergraduate (B.Tech) at Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology, Bhopal in Civil Engineering.
